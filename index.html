About

More than sushi and ramen?
If you are looking for a unique cultural experience in Japan, this is the place for you.
We are happy to teach you the essense of Japanese home cooking.


We will focus on ordinary home cookings that we everyday eat at home,
because that is what we are confident to teach and what you can bring back to your kitchen.
Through the class, you may learn not only about how to cook
but also about the lifestyle and cultural backgroud.


We prefer the menues that you can cook at home for your family or for your friends,
so we are very happy to customise the menu if you have any requests.


Classes will be held privately in a small group.
We appreciate any questions and the communication with you.
We are not professional chefs, but we enjoy cooking!

Menu

A Dashi course

We will learn about dashi(Japanese soup stock),
which is a basis of everything,
and prepare a few dishes using dashi.
If requested, we can arrange "dashi tasting"
to compare soups from different ingredients.

B Home style sushi course

Sushi is a sophisticated art work,
but we can enjoy it in home style.
We will prepare soup as well.

C Wagashi course

We will make a few "kawaii" wagashi(sweets).
Wagashi will be selected depending on the seasons.

D Upon your requests!

What is your ineterest?
We will arrange a special menu for you:)
